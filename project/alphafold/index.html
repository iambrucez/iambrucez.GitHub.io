Using AlphaFold and GNN to Predict Catalytic Efficiency

June 14, 2022

Supervisors: Dr. Gaoyang Li, Prof. Huansheng Cao

Yufan Zhang, Yiyang Sun, Yang Yu, Jinxuan Zhang, Zixu Geng

GitHub

Project Introduction#

This project is part of the research initiative by Dr. Gaoyang Li and Prof. Huansheng Cao. The project aims to use AlphaFold and GNN to predict the catalytic efficiency of several species (e.g., Bacillus subtilis). The project is divided into two parts: the first part is to use AlphaFold to output the structural representation of the enzymes in the species, and the second part is to use GNN to harvest the previous AlphaFold output to predict the catalytic efficiency of all the catalytic processes in the species.

By chance, I joined the project as a research assistant last year. The mentors Dr. Gaoyang Li and Prof. Huansheng Cao of this project already have a clear research direction and the approximate application. My participation is more like helping them put their ideas on the ground and try to optimize the method to a good shape. Specifically, I used Python’s PyTorch and DGL libraries to build a deep learning model, and then through various machine learning optimization methods are used to improve the performance of this model.

The project is still ongoing. Unfortunately, when the work was in full swing, in June this year, we suddenly discovered that a team had used similar ideas to achieve better performance than our model at the time, and had published articles. I decided to leave the project due to the lack of time and the lack of interest in the project. I hope the final report will be released in the future.
